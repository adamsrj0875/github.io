About Me

Release management
across platforms and teams.

I'm a Release and Program Manager with experience in software quality engineering and cross-platform product delivery. I've worked at Apple, TiVo, and Fetch Robotics, in roles spanning release management, QE leadership, and program management.

At Apple, I coordinated the release lifecycle for Maps Client across iOS, macOS, watchOS, tvOS, CarPlay, and visionOS. At TiVo, I led QE readiness across multiple device platforms and managed a lab of 500+ set-top boxes. At Fetch Robotics, I managed releases for FetchCore and restructured the QE test infrastructure into TestRail.

I work closely with Engineering, QA, Product, and executive teams to keep releases on track and surface issues early.
